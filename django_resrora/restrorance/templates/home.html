Your Serene Retreat Awaits

Baranti Banalata Resort (also known as Bonolota Resort) is a serene getaway located in the Purulia district of West Bengal, specifically positioned right beside the tranquil Baranti Lake and Muradi Dam. This resort is highly regarded for its picturesque location, nestled between rolling hills and lush greenery, making it a perfect destination for nature lovers and those seeking a peaceful weekend retreat. The property offers a variety of accommodation options, including comfortable AC deluxe rooms, cottages, and Swiss tents, all designed to provide a rustic yet modern stay experience. Guests often praise the resort for its warm hospitality and authentic Bengali cuisine, which features fresh ingredients, sometimes sourced from their own local farms. Its proximity to the water body allows visitors to enjoy stunning views of the sunrise and sunset over the lake, while the surrounding forests come alive with the vibrant red of Palash flowers during the spring season.

Dine in Elegance and Comfort

Experience a unique blend of modern aesthetics and natural beauty at the Baranti Banalata Resort dining hall. Our spacious and well-lit restaurant features a stunning interior with vibrant, multi-colored LED lighting and artistically designed ceilings that create a warm and inviting atmosphere for every meal. The elegant marble flooring and neatly arranged dining tables provide a premium feel, while the large arched windows offer a breath-taking panoramic view of the lush green fields outside. Whether you are enjoying a traditional Bengali feast or a quiet cup of coffee, the serene environment and the cool breeze from the nearby Baranti Lake make every dining moment truly special. It is the perfect place to relax with family and friends after a day of exploring the scenic hills of Purulia.
